About Me

I'm Abhiram, a software engineer who's spent the last 3+ years building real-world applications that actually matter. What started as curiosity about how websites work during my undergraduate years at BML Munjal University has evolved into a career where I get to solve complex problems every day.

My journey kicked off at Hughes Systique where I cut my teeth on DevOps automation, learning the ropes of CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure management. That foundation proved invaluable when I joined MedPlus, where I spent over 2 years building applications that served 50,000+ users. There, I didn't just write code – I built reusable DataGrid components that are still being used across multiple internal applications, upgraded legacy systems to React 18, and optimized database queries that reduced report generation time by 60%. These weren't just technical achievements; they directly impacted how healthcare professionals access and manage critical information.

Last summer, I took on a Java internship at Global Payments, where I developed REST services for fraud detection systems. Achieving 90% test coverage wasn't just a metric – it was about ensuring financial transactions are secure for millions of users. Now, as I pursue my Master's at Georgia State University, I'm combining academic learning with hands-on experience, working on projects that push the boundaries of what's possible with AI and machine learning.

What drives me isn't just the technology – it's the impact. Whether it's helping students find the right university with my AI-powered FlyEasy platform (which got published at ICCCN 2022), or building food delivery systems that eliminate unnecessary fees (earning the Wadhwani StartMeUp Award), I'm passionate about creating solutions that solve real problems. My toolkit spans from React and Angular on the frontend to Spring Boot and FastAPI on the backend, with extensive experience in databases, DevOps, and mobile development. But more than the tech stack, I bring a mindset of continuous learning, collaborative problem-solving, and a commitment to delivering solutions that work in the real world.

GPTuner - Database Auto-Tuning System

Project Contents

TECHNOLOGY STACK

Large Language Models (LLMs) • Bayesian Optimization • Python • TPC-C/TPC-H Benchmarking • BenchBase • Cloud-scale DBMS • Machine Learning • Database Tuning

GPTuner is an innovative database auto-tuning system designed to revolutionize database performance optimization through the integration of Large Language Models (LLMs) and advanced machine learning techniques. This cutting-edge system automatically reads and processes database tuning manuals using sophisticated LLM algorithms, then applies Bayesian Optimization for intelligent knob-tuning to achieve optimal database configurations without manual intervention.

Performance Optimization & Benchmarking: The system was rigorously benchmarked using industry-standard TPC-C and TPC-H workloads with BenchBase testing framework, achieving remarkable performance improvements including 25% higher throughput and 20% lower query latency compared to traditional manual tuning approaches. These results demonstrate the system's effectiveness in cloud-scale Database Management System (DBMS) environments, making it particularly valuable for enterprise applications requiring high-performance database operations.

Technical Innovation: GPTuner represents a significant advancement in database optimization by combining the power of modern AI technologies with proven optimization algorithms. The system's ability to automatically interpret complex tuning documentation and apply learned knowledge to real-world database configurations showcases the potential of AI-driven infrastructure optimization in enterprise environments.
